Several trades are already on the platform, but it may happen that no pro exactly matches your search. If you can't find exactly the job you're interested in, it's because there is no pro registered for this job yet. But do not panic ! This is what you can do:
1. Explore other professions in the sector and contact the pros
2.Use the search by center of interest to find a range of other professions that could correspond to you.

Help, no one is answering me! First of all, ask yourself these questions: Was my message correctly formulated and without spelling mistakes? Did I use all the polite formulas correctly? Were my questions relevant to the profile of the pro? It may be that professionals do not bother to respond to messages that are poorly worded and not specific enough! It is also possible that the pro is very busy at the moment or on vacation. The pros sometimes respond several days after sending your message. If you still don't have an answer after one or more weeks, don't hesitate to kindly follow up with the pro in writing. While waiting for him to answer you, contact other pros who practice the same profession as him. It's always better to have different opinions, right?

No need to delete your account if you have changed company or profession since your registration: simply update this information! Your new activity will interest young people just as much. Once logged in, go to your dashboard to update your professional experience.

If you are still studying, even on a work-study program, you must register as a young person. If you made a mistake when registering, don't panic! Send an email to: contact@OppCor.com. We can switch your pro account to a young one, but you will then have to give us a date of birth. You can also delete your pro account in “My account” to then create your youth account.
