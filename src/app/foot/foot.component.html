About us

The Opportunity Corner is today the leader in the recruitment of students and young graduates (0 to 5 years of experience) in Tunisia. We already support more than 1,000 companies (mainly startups, very small businesses and SMEs) in all their recruitment of trainees, work-study trainees and junior CDI profiles. Thanks to numerous partnerships, we are in constant contact with thousands of students and junior profiles from the best training courses in commerce, management, digital communication, web development.
